Evaluate the quality of speech systhesis

Thank you for participating in this experiment!

In this experiment, we use a computer to synthesize human speech. The following audio files contain real recordings and synthesized voices. The subjects were asked to listen with headphones (if possible) and gave the following scores according to the quality of each sound file:

  • １（Very bad）
  • ２（Not good）
  • ３（Common）
  • ４（Good）
  • ５（Very good）
